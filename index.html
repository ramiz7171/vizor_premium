Vizor Auto-Sort

🔄 How Auto-Sort Works:

1. Enter your Source data (reference order)

2. Enter your Target data (any order)

3. Hit "Auto-Sort Target" - the third field will show target values rearranged to match source order

4. Compare Source vs Auto-Sorted for perfect matching

Example: Source: [a,b,c] + Target: [b,c,a] → Auto-Sorted: [a,b,c] = Perfect Match! ✅
